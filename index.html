Course Outline

With a foundation in HTML & CSS, students are now ready to dive in deeper into Web Programming. This course goes into detail on responsive design, use of pre-processor tools and PHP. Through a series of hands-on exercises students will learn how to create dynamic web pages.

This course deals with the core technologies of browser based interaction. The use of the pre-processors and PHP greatly increases the scale and speed of the projects that students can be involved with.
